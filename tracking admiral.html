Tracking


Technology plays a major role at Admiral Trucker (Nig) Ltd, from equipment monitoring/tracking to information systems to customer service. Our goal is to drive costs down and efficiency levels up.


In conjunction with Globacomm Telecoms (Glo), Our entire fleet is equipped with communication technology. This gives us instant two- way communications with our drivers for dispatching and directions.


Working with maps and communication devices can request dierctions from their current position to the next stop on their route, or in the unlikely event of a breakdown, to the nearest service facility.


We monitor the engine data constantly to help our service departments to keep our fleet in top performing condition. GPS information from our tractors allow us to track your loads 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
